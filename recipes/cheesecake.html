Cheesecake Recipe

Home Brownies Tiramisu

Description

A nice, refreshing dessert after a long day in the summer heat. Make a cheesecake for youself here!

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese
  • Granulated sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Sour cream
  • Graham cracker crumbs
  • Unsalted butter (melted)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (163°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs and melted butter, then press into the bottom of a springform pan to form the crust.
  3. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth, then add granulated sugar and mix until fully incorporated.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  5. Stir in vanilla extract and sour cream until smooth and creamy.
  6. Pour the filling over the crust and smooth the top.
  7. Bake for 50–60 minutes until the center is almost set.
  8. Turn off the oven, crack the door, and let the cheesecake cool for 1 hour inside the oven.
  9. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight before serving.
